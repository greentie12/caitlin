BLACKENED MAHI MAHI &

MANGO SALSA

DESCRIPTION INGREDIENTS DIRECTIONS INFORMATION

Fresh grilled Mahi Mahi paired with chopped mango and tomato salsa with hints of jalapeno, cilantro, and garlic. Perfect with a side of white or fried ride with a tasteful mix between sweet and spicy flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 mahi mahifillets (4 oz each)
  • PAM Grilling Spray
  • 1 cup of diced tomato
  • 1 medium mango, peeled, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 lime, juiced

Directions

Step 01.

Combine the 1-1/2 teaspoons cumin, garlic powder, dried oregano, ground ginger, smoked paprika, salt and black and red peppers in a small bowl. Stir in olive oil to make a saucy paste; divide mixture in half and set aside on portion.

Step 02.

Brush half of the paste on fish fillets; set aside. Lightly spray grill or grill pan with PAM. Set to high heat, when hot, add fish and cook 3 to 4 minutes per side, turning once.

Step 03.

Meanwhile, combine chopped tomatoes, mango, jalapeno, red onion, cilantro, the one teaspoon cumin, the garlic and lime juice in medium bowl; set aside.

Step 04.

Remove fish from grill once it flakes easily with a fork. Brush remaining paste on cooked fish. Serve with mango salsa.
